What Is NurseTALKS?

NurseTALKS is a ward-focused nursing speaking program designed for nurses and nursing students who need to:

Patient Handover & Documentation

Communicate patient status, care plans, and shift reports clearly in English.

Global Communication

Present at conferences and communicate with international multidisciplinary teams.

The program focuses on real handovers, medication rounds, and patient education ‚Äî not memorizing vocabulary lists.
