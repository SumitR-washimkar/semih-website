PARTNERSHIP MODELS

University Partnerships

For universities and faculties that require measurable speaking outcomes at scale.

We support universities in launching speaking programmes aligned with curriculum objectives and clinical communication standards.

Typical use cases:

  • Medical English speaking labs and electives
  • Faculty-wide speaking placement and progression workflows
  • OET preparation modules (Medicine / Dentistry / Veterinary Science)
  • Blended delivery using coursebooks + AI agents

What we provide:

  • Department-aligned speaking pathways (B1–C1)
  • Custom prompts, scenarios, and rubrics (CEFR-style, adaptable)
  • Staff and student cohort onboarding
  • Implementation guidance and reporting structure

School Partnerships

For schools that need scalable speaking assessment without heavy teacher allocation.

Mr Brown enables standardised speaking evaluation with consistent timing, prompts, and outcomes aligned with CEFR-based school rubrics.

Typical use cases:

  • Placement speaking tests and level streaming
  • Midterm and final speaking exams
  • Progress checks across cohorts
  • Standardised evaluation aligned with internal rubrics

What we provide:

  • CEFR-aligned criteria adaptable to school needs
  • Custom question sets co-designed with academic teams
  • Clear reporting to support placement and progression decisions

Clinic Partnerships

For private clinics that need confident, professional English communication in daily practice.

We support clinics with targeted speaking training focused on patient flow, explanations, and follow-up communication.

Typical use cases:

  • English for patient intake, triage, and follow-up
  • Procedure explanations and post-treatment instructions
  • Dentistry, aesthetic, physiotherapy clinic communication tracks
  • Fast improvement through repeatable micro-sessions

What we provide:

  • Specialty-focused speaking modules
  • Patient-friendly, risk-appropriate communication patterns
  • Flexible access for individual clinicians or teams
