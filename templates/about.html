ABOUT US

Where Medical Language Meets Real Communication

MEDTALKS is a specialist medical communication platform built on one clear principle: medical English must be taught by trained professionals — not casual speakers.
Since 2012, MEDTALKS has been active in the field of professional language education under different names, delivering structured, speaking-focused medical English training across Türkiye, Azerbaijan, and the Middle East.
Long before talk-based platforms became popular, MEDTALKS was already dedicated to structured medical speaking, designed specifically for healthcare professionals who need to function confidently in real clinical and international environments.

WHAT DRIVES US

Our commitment to professional medical communication.

We are driven by a clear belief: medical communication is not casual language — it is a professional skill that directly affects patient trust, safety, and career mobility. MEDTALKS exists to make structured, high-quality medical speaking education accessible to healthcare professionals worldwide, without compromising academic standards.

Mission

To train healthcare professionals to communicate clearly, confidently, and professionally in English by combining academic instruction with structured speaking practice designed for real clinical and international environments.

Vision

To become a globally trusted medical communication platform that supports doctors, dentists, nurses, and pharmacists in delivering their expertise clearly — wherever their careers take them.

Frequently Asked Questions About MEDTALKS

MEDTALKS is a specialist medical communication platform focused on spoken medical English. We train healthcare professionals to communicate clearly, confidently, and professionally in clinical, academic, and international environments.

MEDTALKS does not teach general or conversational English. Our programs are designed exclusively for healthcare professionals and focus on real medical and professional communication, not casual conversation or grammar-heavy instruction.

All MEDTALKS programs are taught by qualified medical English academics. Our instructors hold formal English Language Teaching (ELT) qualifications and specialize in Medical English or English for Specific Purposes (ESP). We do not employ untrained or casual speakers.

MEDTALKS follows a structured, unit-based speaking curriculum. Speaking practice is guided by academic professionals and supported by AI tools designed specifically for medical communication. This ensures accuracy, safety, and professional relevance.

AI tools at MEDTALKS are used to support practice, not replace academic teaching. AI speaking agents simulate clinical and professional scenarios and help learners practice consistently alongside live academic instruction.
